What Is Consulting Club?

One member plays Client and brings a real problem — a strategic decision, an operational knot, a market they're trying to read. The rest form an ad hoc Consulting Team and do the actual work: framing the problem, running the analysis, presenting a recommendation. About 90 minutes, start to finish.

Then comes the part that makes it worth showing up for: a 15-minute debrief where everyone answers Would I hire these people?

No prep decks. No performance reviews. Just smart people doing real work in a room where nothing is at stake except getting better.

Who It's For

You've built expertise over a long career. You know how to think through a hard problem, structure an argument, and give advice worth taking. Consulting Club is where you keep those muscles warm — and for some members, a no-risk way to see what it would actually feel like to hang out a shingle.
