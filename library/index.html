The Library

Frameworks, tools, and session artifacts — organized by the phase of a consulting engagement where you'd reach for them.

The library is open and free. No account, no paywall.
It's organized around how a consulting engagement actually flows — from getting to the real problem, through analysis and strategy, to execution and communication. The Session Kit at the end is for running the sessions themselves.

Scoping & Diagnosis

Getting to the real problem

Before you solve anything, you have to make sure you're solving the right thing. Problem framing, issue trees, root causes, and stakeholder dynamics.

Analysis & Frameworks

The classic toolkit

SWOT, Porter's Five Forces, BCG, 7S, Value Chain, market sizing — the frameworks consultants reach for most, explained well.

Strategy & Planning

From analysis to direction

OKRs, Balanced Scorecard, Blue Ocean, Three Horizons, Wardley Mapping, Business Model Canvas — turning insight into a coherent plan.

Execution & Change

Making it happen

A recommendation no one can implement is worthless. RACI, Kotter, Lean, and prioritization tools for the work after the deck.

Synthesis & Communication

The last mile

The Pyramid Principle, the one-page recommendation, storyboarding, and the So What? test. Often the difference between a good team and one you'd hire.

Session Kit

Ready-to-run session artifacts

Client brief template, role cards, starter cases, the 90-minute agenda, the debrief scorecard, and the facilitator's guide.
